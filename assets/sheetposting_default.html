This is your first post with sheet-posting.

It takes information that you enter into a Google Sheets spreadsheet, and turns it into a customizable blog page and valid RSS feed.

It also supports Markdown!

If this is your blog, edit your spreadsheet to get started. Posts go in the Posts tab, site information goes in the SEO tab, and you have full control over your site's style in the CSS tab.

Then, you can share your blog by copying the URL that appears in your address bar right now, or use the RSS link at the bottom of the page. Show it off to your friends!

If this isn't your blog, make a copy of this spreadsheet to get started!
